Not sure how to get started? Not to worry! Just follow these simple steps to start creating bets and share them with your friends.


Step 1

Make a bet with a friend!

Follow this link or use the top bar to create a new bet.

Step 2

Give your bet key to your friend.

Your friend can access your bet via this link.

Step 3

Your friend can now use the key to place their side of the bet.

E-mail yourself the bet URL so you can keep track of the bet!



That's all there is to it! Go ahead and try making your first bet if you haven't already. If you have any questions, check out our FAQ page.
